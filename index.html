CarGo USA Sales Performance

This Dashboard was built on a structured sales dataset containing detailed information on each vehicle transaction, including pricing, vehicle attributes (company, model, body style, engine, transmission, and colour), customer demographics, and dealer location data. The analysis revealed that strong year-over-year revenue growth was largely driven by increased unit sales rather than higher average prices, highlighting important trade-offs between volume growth and margin optimisation. The final deliverable was a two-page interactive Power BI dashboard designed for both executive-level overview and detailed analysis.

Meta Ad Sales Performance Analysis

This project delivers a data-driven performance analysis of Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns using Meta Ads data. By designing a star-schema data model and building an interactive Power BI dashboard, the analysis uncovers how strong top-of-funnel performance (high reach, CTR, and engagement) fails to translate into bottom-of-funnel conversions. The findings reveal exceptionally high engagement (CTR: 11.76%, Engagement Rate: 13.56%) driven primarily by female users aged 18–32, with Stories and Image ads outperforming other formats. However, a low purchase rate (0.60%) exposes a significant conversion drop-off, highlighting opportunities for landing page optimisation, audience refinement, and budget reallocation.
